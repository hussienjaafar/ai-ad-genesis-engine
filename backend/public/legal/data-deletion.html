Data Deletion Policy

In compliance with privacy regulations and platform policies, [COMPANY NAME] provides this data deletion mechanism for users and platform partners. This page explains how you can request deletion of your data from our systems.

For Platform Users

If you are a user of our platform and wish to delete your account and associated data:

  1. Log into your account
  2. Go to Account Settings
  3. Select "Delete Account" at the bottom of the page
  4. Confirm your decision

Your account and all associated personal data will be permanently deleted within 30 days. During this period, your account will be deactivated and inaccessible.

Meta/Facebook Platform Data

If you've authorized our application to access your Meta/Facebook advertising data and wish to delete this connection:

  1. Log into your account
  2. Go to Platform Integration
  3. Click "Disconnect" next to Meta/Facebook

Additionally, you can revoke our application's access from your Meta/Facebook settings:

  1. Go to Facebook Business Integrations
  2. Find our app "[YOUR APP NAME]"
  3. Click "Remove" to revoke access

Meta Data Deletion Request

This endpoint serves as a callback URL for Meta's Data Deletion Request feature. When Meta sends a user ID deletion request, we will permanently delete all data associated with that user ID from our systems.

API Endpoint:

POST /api/data-deletion/meta

Required Parameters:

  • user_id: The Meta user ID to be deleted
  • confirmation_code: A unique code for tracking the deletion request

Manual Data Deletion Request

If you prefer to directly request data deletion or have any difficulties with the above methods, please contact our Data Protection Officer:

Email: dpo@[YOUR-DOMAIN].com

Subject: Data Deletion Request

Please include:

  • Your full name
  • Email address associated with your account
  • Brief description of the data you wish to have deleted

We will respond to your request within 30 days and provide confirmation once the deletion is complete.
